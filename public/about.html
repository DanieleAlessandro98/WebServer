About Me

I have a degree in Computer Science from the University of Bari Aldo Moro.

Since my childhood I have always been fascinated by computers and technology. My curiosity, combined with an interest in video games, led me to discover what was behind these games. This led me to explore the world of computer science and to take my first steps in programming.

Over the years I have had the opportunity to develop and expand my skills. I have participated in several projects, both in teams and individually, where I have applied and refined my knowledge following agile methodologies. I am passionate about open source projects where I can share experiences and learn from others. I am constantly striving to improve my skills and grow professionally as a software engineer.

During my university career I had the opportunity to put my knowledge into practice. I wrote code to design, implement and maintain systems for a multiplayer MMO game based on a client-server architecture.

In my free time I like to be in contact with nature and animals. The relationship with the environment relaxes me and gives me a rejuvenating break from the digital world.
